# What Makes This Defensible

**Lineage.** Founded by Dr. Hun Lye, a Drikung Kagyu practitioner. Every product is selected for practice use, not aesthetics.

**Direct Nepal Sourcing.** 50--80% gross margins through established artisan relationships. No middlemen.

**Practitioner Audience.** Customers are serious practitioners -- not "wellness tourists." This drives repeat purchases and word-of-mouth.

**559-Product Catalog.** Decades of curation: mala, thangka, singing bowls, dharma texts, ritual implements.

**Competitive Landscape:**

| Competitor | Est. Revenue | TS Advantage |
|---|---|---|
| DharmaCrafts | $3--8M | TS has deeper lineage authenticity |
| Buddha Groove | $5M+ | TS serves practitioners, not home-decor buyers |
| DharmaShop | Mid-range | TS has broader catalog, direct sourcing |

# The Opportunity

Every standard growth channel is untouched.

| Channel | Status | Why It Matters |
|---|---|---|
| Email Marketing | Never launched | Religious/spiritual audiences: **55.7% open rate** (highest of any industry) |
| SEO / Content | Minimal | "Singing bowls": 40--60K/mo searches. "Mala beads": 10--20K/mo |
| Paid Ads | Zero spend | Proven product-market fit at $0 ad spend = strong unit economics for paid |

| Etsy | Not listed | Natural fit for handcrafted, artisan goods |
| Wholesale | Minimal | Dharma centers, retreat centers, yoga studios |

Comparable businesses in this space do **$3--8M+ in revenue** with less authentic sourcing.

# Path to Profitability

Negative profit in Y1--2 is intentional -- we are investing in growth infrastructure.

| | **Year 1** | **Year 2** | **Year 3** |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Revenue** | $167K | $725K | $1,121K |
| **Profit** | -$156K | -$99K | +$4K |
| **Marketing Spend** | $94K | $248K | $318K |
| **Ending Cash** | $94K | $95K | $149K |

**Key dynamics:**
- Y1 losses reflect growth investment with a deliberate marketing ramp -- ad spend starts at zero in June, scales gradually through the learning period
- Y2 losses narrow as revenue scales and marketing efficiency improves
- Y3 crosses to profitability with $149K cash position
- Cash stays positive every month across all three years

# Growth Roadmap

**Phase 1: Foundation** (June 2026 -- May 2027)
- Launch email marketing (55.7% open rates in this audience)
- SEO for high-volume keywords (singing bowls, mala beads)
- Begin paid advertising (learning period June--September, full scale October+)
- Launch on Etsy (August 2026)
- Build quarterly subscription box
- Begin Rare/Antique/Premium sourcing (Jothi in Kathmandu)

**Phase 2: Scale** (June 2027 -- May 2028)
- Amazon marketplace launch
- TS Travels: first curated Nepal trip
- Premium tier ramps to 1.5 sales/month
- Wholesale expansion to dharma centers and retreat centers

**Phase 3: Maturity** (June 2028 -- May 2029)
- All product lines operating at scale
- Subscription growing to $124K/yr
- Marketing efficiency improves as brand recognition builds
- Profitability achieved ($4K EBITDA)
