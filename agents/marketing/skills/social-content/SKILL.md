# Social Content Management Skill

## Overview
Build brand presence and community through consistent, authentic organic social media content across platforms aligned with business objectives.

## Key Sections

### Social Media Strategy
- Define social media role in marketing strategy
- Identify core social platforms (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, etc.)
- Define target audience by platform
- Set growth and engagement targets
- Plan content themes and pillars
- Establish brand voice and tone

### Platform Strategy
- Instagram: Visual storytelling, community building, product showcasing
- Facebook: Community, longer-form content, events
- TikTok: Authentic, educational, trend-responsive content
- Pinterest: Inspirational, how-to, aesthetic content
- LinkedIn: Industry insights, thought leadership (if applicable)
- YouTube: Long-form video, tutorials, brand storytelling

### Content Pillars and Themes
- Educational content (how-to, practice tips, spiritual teachings)
- Product and feature showcases
- Customer stories and testimonials
- Behind-the-scenes and company culture
- Inspirational and motivational content
- Seasonal and timely content

### Content Calendar Planning
- Plan content calendar 4-6 weeks in advance
- Align with product launches and campaigns
- Incorporate seasonal and cultural events (tibetan-calendar)
- Balance content types and pillars
- Plan content frequency by platform
- Coordinate with other marketing initiatives

### Visual Content Creation
- Source or create visual assets for posts
- Maintain consistent visual brand identity
- Optimize images for each platform
- Use brand colors, fonts, and design elements
- Create graphics and text overlays
- Maintain asset library

### Video Content Strategy
- Plan video content themes
- Create short-form vertical videos (Reels, TikToks)
- Develop tutorial and educational videos
- Plan behind-the-scenes content
- Optimize video for each platform
- Track video performance

### Community Engagement
- Respond to comments and messages timely
- Build relationships with followers
- Encourage user-generated content
- Engage with community content
- Host Q&As and interactive sessions
- Build loyal community members

### Hashtag Strategy
- Research relevant hashtags
- Build hashtag sets for content types
- Use mix of popular and niche hashtags
- Create branded hashtag
- Monitor hashtag trends
- Optimize hashtag strategy

### Posting Schedule and Timing
- Determine optimal posting times by platform
- Maintain consistent posting frequency
- Test posting times for engagement
- Coordinate posting across platforms
- Use scheduling tools for efficiency
- Monitor timing impact on engagement

### Performance Monitoring
- Track follower growth and engagement rate
- Monitor post reach and impressions
- Analyze engagement by content type
- Track website traffic from social
- Monitor sentiment and brand mentions
- Identify top-performing content

### Community Building
- Build loyalty programs or exclusive content
- Create challenges or campaigns
- Encourage sharing and tagging
- Build partnerships with other creators
- Host live sessions and events
- Foster sense of community

### Trends and Innovation
- Monitor platform trends and features
- Test new content formats
- Participate in relevant trends authentically
- Stay current with platform changes
- Experiment with emerging platforms
- Document learnings and best practices

### Analytics and Reporting
- Generate weekly social performance reports
- Track metrics by platform and content type
- Calculate engagement rate and growth
- Analyze audience insights and demographics
- Identify content performance patterns
- Share insights and recommendations

## Success Metrics
- Follower growth rate
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Reach and impressions
- Website traffic from social
- Conversion rate from social
- Community sentiment and brand perception
