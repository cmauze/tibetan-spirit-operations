# Creative Library Skill

## Overview
Build and maintain a centralized, organized library of approved creative assets and templates to ensure brand consistency and accelerate campaign execution.

## Key Sections

### Creative Asset Organization
- Organize assets by category (incense, statues, textiles, etc.)
- Tag assets by channel (social, email, search, etc.)
- Label assets by use case (awareness, conversion, seasonal, etc.)
- Version control creative assets and updates
- Archive outdated or retired creative

### Ad Copy Library
- Curate high-performing ad headlines and body copy
- Build copy variations by audience and channel
- Create copy templates for different ad formats
- Document copy performance and learnings
- Build reusable copy blocks and phrases
- Organize by theme and campaign type

### Visual Creative Assets
- Organize product photography by SKU
- Maintain lifestyle and contextual images
- Store brand graphics and design elements
- Maintain color palette and brand standards
- Organize icons, illustrations, and graphics
- Document image licensing and usage rights

### Email and SMS Templates
- Store email campaign templates
- Build responsive email designs
- Create subject line variations
- Store SMS message templates
- Organize templates by campaign type and audience
- Document template performance data

### Landing Page Templates
- Maintain conversion-focused landing page designs
- Store form templates and variations
- Create product showcase page designs
- Organize by product type and audience
- Document landing page performance

### Social Media Content Templates
- Build reusable social post templates
- Store carousel and video templates
- Create hashtag sets and captions
- Organize stories, reels, and video templates
- Document best-performing formats

### Design System and Brand Standards
- Document brand guidelines and standards
- Maintain color palette and typography
- Store approved logos and mark variations
- Define spacing and layout standards
- Create design templates and specs
- Document brand voice and messaging

### Approval Workflow
- Define approval process for new creative
- Document approval requirements by channel
- Create review checklist for brand compliance
- Assign approval authority and responsibilities
- Track approval status and version history

### Performance Documentation
- Track creative performance metrics
- Document A/B test results and learnings
- Record high-performing creative variations
- Analyze creative effectiveness by channel
- Share performance insights with team
- Use data to guide creative development

### Creative Asset Management
- Organize and tag for easy discovery
- Maintain searchable asset database
- Version all creative updates
- Document asset creation date and source
- Track asset usage and retirement
- Plan regular library maintenance and cleanup

## Dependencies
- Integrates with: all marketing channel skills, campaign-architecture
- References: brand-guidelines for design standards

## Success Metrics
- Creative asset library size and coverage
- Time to create new campaigns (using templates vs. from scratch)
- Campaign performance by creative asset
- Creative reuse rate
- Brand compliance rate in creative
