# Product Photography Standards Skill

## Overview
Define and enforce photography standards across channels to ensure consistent, high-quality product images that drive conversion and trust.

## Key Sections

### Photography Standards by Channel
- D2C (Shopify): Brand-consistent lifestyle and detailed shots
- Etsy: 1000x1000px minimum, multiple angles, lifestyle context
- Amazon: Specific sizing, white background requirement, angle requirements
- Social media: Mobile-optimized, attention-grabbing, various aspect ratios

### D2C Photography Standards
- Minimum resolution and file format specs
- Brand aesthetic and color grading guidelines
- Number and types of product images required
- Lifestyle photography requirements
- Usage and context for each image type

### Etsy Photography Standards
- Minimum 1000x1000px resolution
- File format and quality specifications
- First image critical importance
- Multiple angle requirements
- Lifestyle photography recommendations
- Seasonal and themed photography

### Amazon Photography Standards
- White background requirement
- Main image specifications (1000x1000px minimum)
- Multiple image requirements and sequence
- Technical specifications and file formats
- Infographic and lifestyle guidelines
- Compliance requirements

### Quality Control Standards
- Focus and sharpness requirements
- Color accuracy and consistency
- Lighting standards
- Product presentation standards
- Branding and watermark requirements
- Defect and damage standards

### Asset Management
- Organize product photography by SKU
- Version control for updated photos
- Archive old photography
- Track photo dates and updates
- Maintain high-resolution masters
- Manage licensing and rights

### Photo Refresh and Updates
- Schedule photography refreshes
- Update for seasonal variations
- Capture new product angles
- Refresh lifestyle photography
- Monitor for photo aging or degradation

### Compliance and Audit
- Audit listings for photo compliance
- Identify missing or substandard photos
- Verify channel-specific requirements
- Check for expired promotions or outdated products
- Document standards for vendors

## Dependencies
- Integrates with: amazon-listing-optimization, etsy-content-optimization, cross-channel-parity
- References: brand-guidelines for brand standards

## Success Metrics
- Percentage of listings with compliant images
- Photo quality consistency score
- Image-to-conversion rate impact
- Customer satisfaction with product photos
