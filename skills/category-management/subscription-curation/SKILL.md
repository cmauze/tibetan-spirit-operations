# Subscription Curation Skill

## Overview
Curate quarterly Incense Club subscription boxes with thoughtful product selection, seasonal themes, and customer delight in mind.

## Key Sections

### Subscription Strategy and Goals
- Define subscription value proposition and positioning
- Establish customer retention and lifetime value targets
- Set pricing strategy and margin requirements
- Plan quarterly theme strategy
- Align with brand values and customer expectations

### Quarterly Theme Planning
- Align with tibetan-calendar and seasonal events (Losar, Vesak, etc.)
- Research theme relevance and customer interest
- Plan theme story and narrative
- Coordinate with marketing and messaging
- Plan announcement and promotion timing

### Product Selection Process
- Define product mix for each quarter (types and quantities)
- Consider product variety and discovery
- Balance popular vs. exploratory products
- Source new or limited products for boxes
- Ensure seasonal relevance of selections
- Plan product sourcing and inventory allocation

### Curation Criteria
- Customer preference analysis and feedback
- Product quality and brand fit assessment
- Margin and cost structure optimization
- Differentiation from retail offerings
- Discovery of new products for subscribers
- Balance of core incenses with complementary items

### Packaging and Presentation
- Design box presentation and unboxing experience
- Plan packaging materials and sustainability
- Include product information cards and narratives
- Curate themed materials or spiritual items
- Plan branded packaging elements
- Consider seasonal or limited edition touches

### Subscriber Communication
- Announce quarterly theme and contents in advance
- Share product stories and inspiration
- Include usage suggestions and practice guidance
- Gather subscriber feedback on selections
- Plan promotional messaging for new subscribers

### Inventory and Logistics Planning
- Forecast box demand and subscriber growth
- Reserve inventory for subscription boxes
- Coordinate with fulfillment teams
- Plan shipping timeline and carrier
- Track fulfillment accuracy and issues

### Performance Monitoring
- Track subscriber retention and churn rate
- Monitor subscription revenue and profitability
- Gather subscriber satisfaction feedback
- Analyze product preferences and reactions
- Identify most popular and well-received items
- Track acquisition of new subscribers

### Continuous Improvement
- Document curation decisions and rationale
- Gather subscriber feedback and reviews
- Analyze retention impact of curation choices
- Plan improvements for upcoming quarters
- Share learnings across team

## Dependencies
- Integrates with: collection-management, tibetan-calendar, assortment-planning
- References: product-knowledge for product selection

## Success Metrics
- Subscriber retention rate
- Subscriber satisfaction score
- Box profit margin
- Subscriber acquisition rate
- Customer lifetime value
