# Marketplace Expansion Skill

## Overview
Systematically evaluate and pilot new sales channels using a decision framework that balances opportunity, feasibility, and strategic alignment.

## Key Sections

### Marketplace Opportunity Assessment
- Identify emerging and established marketplaces
- Research marketplace audience and fit
- Assess market size and growth rate
- Evaluate customer overlap with existing channels
- Identify platform-specific advantages

### Channel Viability Framework
- Define criteria for channel evaluation (fee structure, audience, growth, etc.)
- Assess alignment with strategic goals
- Evaluate technical requirements and integration
- Estimate resource and investment requirements
- Calculate potential return on investment

### Marketplace Fee and Economics Analysis
- Research platform fee structures (commission, listing, payment, storage, etc.)
- Calculate total cost of operation by marketplace
- Model profitability scenarios by volume
- Compare economics across channels
- Identify minimum volume thresholds for profitability

### Operational Requirements Assessment
- Evaluate listing and content requirements
- Assess inventory integration complexity
- Review fulfillment requirements and limitations
- Identify customer service expectations
- Evaluate compliance and regulatory requirements
- Plan technology and integration effort

### Competitive Landscape Analysis
- Assess competitor presence in marketplace
- Evaluate competitive intensity and positioning
- Identify differentiation opportunities
- Analyze pricing and promotional norms
- Assess customer review/ratings expectations

### Pilot Launch Planning
- Design pilot program scope and timeline
- Plan product assortment and inventory
- Develop content and listing strategy
- Plan marketing and promotion
- Define success metrics and KPIs
- Schedule review and decision point

### Launch Execution
- Set up merchant account and store
- Develop content and product listings
- Integrate with inventory management
- Plan fulfillment and operations
- Execute marketing and launch promotion
- Monitor initial performance

### Performance Monitoring and Evaluation
- Track sales volume and revenue
- Monitor profit margins and profitability
- Measure customer satisfaction and reviews
- Track operational metrics (fulfillment, returns)
- Assess resource utilization
- Document learnings and improvements

### Growth or Discontinuation Decision
- Evaluate pilot against success criteria
- Assess strategic value beyond financial metrics
- Decide to scale, continue at current level, or discontinue
- Plan resource allocation for decision
- Document decision rationale and learnings

## Dependencies
- Integrates with: pricing-strategy, cross-channel-parity, inventory-management
- References: product-knowledge for marketplace fit

## Success Metrics
- Marketplace revenue and growth rate
- Profitability and margin by marketplace
- Customer acquisition cost
- Pilot launch to decision timeline
- Percentage of marketplace expansions reaching profitability
