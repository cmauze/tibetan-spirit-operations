# Category Portfolio Management Skill

## Overview
Assess and optimize the product portfolio across categories using data-driven analysis of performance, lifecycle, and strategic fit.

## Key Sections

### Portfolio Segmentation (80/20 Analysis)
- Identify top 20% of products driving 80% of revenue
- Segment portfolio by contribution to profit
- Track performance by category and subcategory
- Identify stars, cash cows, and question marks
- Analyze distribution of SKUs vs. revenue/profit

### Category Performance Metrics
- Revenue contribution by category
- Profit contribution and margin by category
- Growth rate by category (YoY trend)
- Inventory turnover by category
- Return rate and quality issues by category

### Product Lifecycle Analysis
- Identify new products (launch phase)
- Track growth stage products (ramping)
- Identify mature/stable products
- Recognize declining/declining products
- Assess products ready for discontinuation

### Strategic Category Assessment
- Evaluate strategic importance of categories
- Assess fit with brand positioning
- Consider customer expectations
- Evaluate competitive positioning
- Analyze seasonal and trend sensitivity

### Portfolio Gaps and Opportunities
- Identify underserved customer needs
- Assess white space for new products
- Identify adjacent category expansion opportunities
- Evaluate complementary product gaps
- Consider complementary product bundling

### Rationalization Analysis
- Identify low-performing SKUs for discontinuation
- Calculate rationalization ROI (space, complexity savings)
- Assess customer impact of discontinuation
- Plan phase-out and inventory liquidation
- Monitor customer feedback on changes

### Inventory Optimization
- Analyze inventory levels by lifecycle stage
- Optimize stock levels for mature vs. new products
- Identify slow-moving inventory issues
- Recommend inventory write-offs or clearance
- Balance freshness with working capital

### Reporting and Recommendations
- Generate portfolio health dashboard
- Present category performance summaries
- Recommend SKU additions or discontinuations
- Propose category strategy adjustments
- Track portfolio composition changes over time

## Success Metrics
- Portfolio revenue growth
- Profit margin percentage
- Portfolio complexity (SKU count)
- Revenue concentration (80/20 ratio)
- Category health score
