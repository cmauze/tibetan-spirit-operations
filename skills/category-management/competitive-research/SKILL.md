# Competitive Research Skill

## Overview
Systematically monitor competitors' activities, pricing, positioning, and product strategy to inform business decisions and identify market gaps.

## Key Sections

### Competitor Identification
- Identify primary competitors (direct and indirect)
- Segment by channel (D2C, Etsy, Amazon, marketplace)
- Track emerging competitors
- Categorize by business model and positioning
- Monitor competitor viability and market presence

### Pricing Monitoring
- Track competitor pricing by product
- Monitor price changes and promotions
- Compare pricing across channels
- Analyze pricing strategy patterns
- Calculate price positioning relative to competitors
- Track discount frequency and depth

### Product Assortment Analysis
- Catalog competitor product offerings
- Track new product launches
- Identify product gaps and opportunities
- Monitor product lifecycle stages
- Analyze product quality and positioning
- Track SKU expansion or rationalization

### Marketing and Messaging
- Monitor competitor campaigns and promotions
- Analyze messaging and positioning language
- Track advertising spend estimates
- Monitor social media and content strategy
- Identify marketing themes and seasonal focus
- Evaluate promotional timing

### Customer Experience Analysis
- Assess website design and usability
- Analyze checkout process and friction
- Evaluate customer service responsiveness
- Monitor reviews and ratings
- Track return policies and guarantees
- Assess shipping and delivery experience

### Market Positioning
- Analyze competitor brand positioning
- Track target audience messaging
- Evaluate value proposition clarity
- Monitor loyalty programs and retention tactics
- Assess community building efforts
- Track partnership and collaboration strategies

### Weekly Scan Execution
- Schedule regular monitoring activities
- Document findings in consistent format
- Track changes from previous scans
- Identify significant shifts or new activities
- Generate weekly summary reports
- Escalate strategic findings

### Insights and Recommendations
- Synthesize findings into strategic insights
- Identify threats and opportunities
- Recommend strategic responses
- Benchmark against competitor performance
- Inform pricing and assortment decisions
- Guide marketing strategy

- Weekly scan completion rate
- Insight actionability
- Strategy influence from research
- Time to identify market shifts
