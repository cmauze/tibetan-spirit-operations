# Wholesale Strategy Skill

## Overview
Build and manage wholesale relationships with Dharma centers and temples through strategic pricing, supply chain, and service differentiation.

## Key Sections

### Wholesale Opportunity Assessment
- Identify potential wholesale customers (Dharma centers, temples, retail partners)
- Evaluate wholesale market opportunity
- Assess customer needs and order patterns
- Evaluate partner strategic fit
- Plan phased wholesale expansion

### Wholesale Pricing Strategy
- Develop wholesale pricing tier structure
- Calculate minimum order values and volume breaks
- Establish margin requirements for wholesalers
- Account for wholesale delivery and support costs
- Plan pricing competitiveness vs. D2C
- Build wholesale price list and terms

### Product Assortment for Wholesale
- Identify products suitable for wholesale
- Determine popular items for bulk purchasing
- Plan bulk packaging and sizing options
- Define minimum order quantities by product
- Build wholesale product catalog
- Plan new wholesale product development

### Wholesale Sales and Relationship Management
- Identify and recruit wholesale customers
- Onboard new wholesale partners
- Establish regular communication and support
- Plan and manage bulk orders
- Provide wholesale customer service
- Build long-term relationships

### Supply Chain and Fulfillment
- Plan wholesale order fulfillment processes
- Coordinate with inventory-management
- Establish delivery timelines and logistics
- Plan bulk packing and shipping
- Manage freight and handling costs
- Track wholesale order fulfillment

### Bulk Order Management
- Process wholesale purchase orders
- Manage order customization requests
- Track production and delivery status
- Handle questions and customer service
- Ensure on-time delivery
- Manage order accuracy and quality

### Volume and Forecasting
- Develop sales forecasts by wholesale customer
- Track wholesale revenue trends
- Monitor wholesale profit margins
- Plan inventory allocation for wholesale
- Coordinate with assortment-planning for wholesale needs
- Manage wholesale seasonality

### Wholesale Support and Service
- Provide product information and usage guidance
- Support retail marketing and point-of-sale
- Track wholesale customer satisfaction
- Gather feedback for product development
- Manage disputes and returns
- Build strategic partnerships

### Marketing and Development
- Identify emerging wholesale opportunities
- Support wholesale marketing initiatives
- Coordinate with wholesaler promotional plans
- Share marketing materials and resources
- Build wholesale community and relationships
- Plan wholesale trade shows and events

## Dependencies
- Integrates with: inventory-management, pricing-strategy, assortment-planning
- References: product-knowledge for wholesale products

## Success Metrics
- Wholesale revenue and growth rate
- Number of wholesale customers
- Average order size and frequency
- Wholesale profit margin
- Customer retention and repeat order rate
