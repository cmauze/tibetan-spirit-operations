# Assortment Planning Skill

## Overview
Strategically plan product assortment aligned with seasonal demand, customer preferences, and business objectives to maximize relevance and sell-through.

## Key Sections

### Demand Analysis
- Analyze historical sales by season
- Identify seasonal trends and patterns
- Track customer search behavior by season
- Monitor category demand shifts
- Forecast demand by season and product
- Identify emerging trends and opportunities

### Seasonal Planning Calendar
- Align with tibetan-calendar events (Losar, Vesak, etc.)
- Plan assortment for each season in advance
- Schedule product launches and promotions
- Plan product phase-out timing
- Coordinate with inventory and purchasing
- Build seasonal collection calendars

### Assortment Gap Analysis
- Identify products missing for seasonal needs
- Assess coverage by product type and customer segment
- Evaluate breadth and depth of assortment
- Identify unique or differentiated opportunities
- Recommend new product additions
- Plan complementary product bundling

### Inventory Level Planning
- Project needed inventory by season
- Balance depth vs. breadth of assortment
- Account for lead times and supplier capacity
- Plan for clearance of excess seasonal inventory
- Coordinate with supplier-communication
- Manage working capital implications

### Category Mix Optimization
- Determine optimal category mix by season
- Plan allocation of shelf space (physical and digital)
- Balance high-margin vs. high-volume products
- Plan premium tier offerings
- Structure entry-level and gift assortment
- Optimize for customer journey

### New Product Planning
- Identify new product opportunities
- Plan launch timing and season
- Assess cannibalization risk
- Plan marketing support for launches
- Evaluate success metrics and tracking
- Plan product lifecycle management

### Promotional Assortment
- Plan promotional assortment bundles
- Coordinate with promotion-strategy
- Assess margin impact of promotions
- Plan bundled pricing strategy
- Track promotional effectiveness
- Plan clearance assortment

### Execution and Monitoring
- Track assortment plan vs. actual
- Monitor sell-through rates by season
- Identify underperforming assortment choices
- Adjust plans based on performance
- Document lessons for next season
- Build forecasting accuracy

## Dependencies
- Integrates with: category-portfolio, competitive-research, tibetan-calendar, promotion-strategy
- References: product-knowledge for assortment details

## Success Metrics
- Assortment relevance score
- Seasonal sell-through rate
- Inventory turns by season
- Forecast accuracy
- Customer satisfaction with seasonal assortment
