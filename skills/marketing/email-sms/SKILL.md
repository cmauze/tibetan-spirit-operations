# Email and SMS Marketing Skill

## Overview
Build and execute email and SMS marketing campaigns in Klaviyo to drive repeat purchases, build customer relationships, and maximize customer lifetime value.

## Key Sections

### Email and SMS Strategy
- Define email/SMS role in customer lifecycle
- Plan communications by customer journey stage
- Balance promotional vs. educational content
- Set frequency targets and cadence
- Plan seasonal campaign calendar
- Establish unsubscribe and preference management

### Audience Segmentation
- Build core customer segments (new, repeat, VIP, at-risk)
- Create segment criteria and definitions
- Build behavioral segments (purchase history, engagement)
- Create demographic segments
- Plan dynamic segment updates
- Manage segment overlap and exclusions

### List and Preference Management
- Maintain email and SMS subscriber lists
- Implement double opt-in for email
- Manage unsubscribe and preference pages
- Track compliance with email regulations
- Segment by communication preferences
- Monitor list health and engagement

### Automated Flows Strategy
- Welcome series for new subscribers
- Post-purchase order confirmation and follow-up
- Abandoned cart recovery
- Browse abandonment recovery
- Win-back campaigns for inactive customers
- Re-engagement campaigns
- VIP/loyalty customer flows

### Campaign Planning and Content
- Plan promotional campaign calendar
- Create seasonal and holiday campaigns
- Develop educational content campaigns
- Build product launch campaigns
- Design review/survey request campaigns
- Plan birthday and anniversary campaigns

### Email Template Design and Optimization
- Create responsive email templates
- Optimize for mobile viewing
- Design clear call-to-actions
- Build template library for reuse
- A/B test subject lines and copy
- Test design variations

### SMS Content Strategy
- Craft concise SMS messaging
- Plan SMS offers and promotions
- Use SMS for time-sensitive offers
- Balance marketing and transactional SMS
- Test messaging variations
- Monitor SMS performance

### Segmentation and Personalization
- Use Klaviyo segmentation features
- Personalize content by customer attributes
- Implement dynamic product recommendations
- Personalize offers based on behavior
- Test personalization impact
- Refine personalization approach

### Performance Monitoring and Analytics
- Track email open rate and click-through rate
- Monitor conversion rate and revenue
- Calculate email ROI and LTV impact
- Track SMS response and conversion
- Analyze performance by segment
- Monitor unsubscribe and complaint rates

### List Growth and Quality
- Plan email list growth tactics
- Implement signup forms and pop-ups
- Monitor list quality metrics
- Remove inactive subscribers
- Re-engage inactive segments
- Track list growth rate

### Compliance and Deliverability
- Maintain email authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
- Monitor bounce and complaint rates
- Maintain sender reputation
- Follow email law requirements (CAN-SPAM, GDPR)
- Monitor spam folder placement
- Test deliverability regularly

### Reporting and Optimization
- Generate weekly email performance reports
- Calculate metrics by campaign type
- Identify high and low performers
- Recommend optimization opportunities
- Track trends and patterns
- Document insights and learnings

- Email open rate
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Conversion rate
- Email revenue per subscriber
- SMS conversion rate
- List growth rate
