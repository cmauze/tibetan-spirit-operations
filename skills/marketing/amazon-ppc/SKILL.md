# Amazon PPC Management Skill

## Overview
Execute and optimize Amazon advertising campaigns across Sponsored Products, Sponsored Brands, and Sponsored Display to increase product visibility and sales.

## Key Sections

### Amazon Advertising Strategy
- Define Amazon PPC role in overall ecommerce strategy
- Plan campaigns by product category and objectives
- Allocate budget between Sponsored Products, Brands, and Display
- Set ROAS and profitability targets
- Plan seasonal campaign adjustments
- Align with inventory-management for stock availability

### Sponsored Products Campaign Strategy
- Identify high-potential ASINs for campaigns
- Plan campaigns by product, brand, or category
- Define target ROAS and profitability thresholds
- Plan automatic vs. manual targeting campaigns
- Structure ad groups for management and optimization
- Set budgets and daily spend targets

### Keyword Research for Amazon
- Research keywords relevant to products
- Use Amazon search bar and keyword tools
- Analyze search volume and competition
- Identify high-intent and long-tail keywords
- Build negative keyword lists
- Monitor competitor keyword strategies

### Bidding Strategy and Management
- Select bidding strategies (dynamic, fixed, auto-scaling)
- Set initial bid levels by keyword/target
- Implement bid adjustments by placement
- Monitor ACoS (Advertising Cost of Sale)
- Optimize bids based on performance data
- Scale bids on high-performing keywords

### Sponsored Brands Campaign Strategy
- Plan Sponsored Brands campaigns for brand awareness
- Create compelling brand messaging and creative
- Develop brand keywords and category targeting
- Use store visibility features
- Plan headline and promotional messaging
- Optimize for brand-related searches

### Sponsored Display Campaign Strategy
- Plan Sponsored Display for retargeting and awareness
- Target competitor products and audiences
- Use product and category targeting options
- Optimize creative and messaging
- Monitor performance vs. targets
- Test audience expansion strategies

### Campaign Monitoring and Optimization
- Monitor campaigns daily for performance
- Track key metrics (impressions, clicks, spend, sales, ACoS)
- Analyze performance by keyword, campaign, and ASIN
- Identify high and low performers
- Pause underperforming keywords/targets
- Increase spend on winning campaigns

### Keyword Performance Analysis
- Analyze search term reports for insights
- Identify high-volume, low-converting keywords
- Find new high-performing keywords
- Adjust bids based on keyword performance
- Add converting search terms as keywords
- Monitor keyword trend changes

### Competitive Positioning
- Monitor competitor bids and messaging
- Identify competitive threats and opportunities
- Test competitive keywords and targeting
- Assess premium placement opportunities
- Optimize for first-page positioning
- Manage bid wars strategically

### Profitability Management
- Track profitability by ASIN and campaign
- Calculate true ROI including Amazon fees
- Monitor ACoS vs. profitability targets
- Pause unprofitable ASINs or keywords
- Set minimum profit thresholds
- Report profitability to leadership

### Reporting and Performance Analysis
- Generate weekly campaign performance reports
- Track metrics by campaign, keyword, and ASIN
- Calculate ROAS, ACoS, and profit metrics
- Identify trends and seasonal patterns
- Share insights and recommendations
- Document optimization decisions and results
