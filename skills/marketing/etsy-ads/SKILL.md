# Etsy Ads Management Skill

## Overview
Plan, execute, and optimize Etsy's native advertising platform to increase product visibility within Etsy search and drive sales.

## Key Sections

### Etsy Ads Strategy
- Define Etsy Ads role in ecommerce strategy
- Identify products suitable for advertising
- Plan budget allocation within Etsy budget
- Set performance targets and ROAS goals
- Plan seasonal campaign adjustments
- Align with inventory levels for stock availability

### Promoted Listings Selection
- Identify highest-potential products for promotion
- Analyze product profitability and margins
- Consider product competition and visibility
- Select products with strong listings and reviews
- Balance between new and established products
- Rotate products based on performance

### Bid Management
- Understand Etsy's bidding model and mechanics
- Set initial bid levels for products
- Monitor performance and adjust bids
- Identify optimal bid levels for profitability
- Test bid increases for scaling
- Manage cost per click within targets

### Daily Budget Setting
- Define total daily budget for advertising
- Allocate budget across product categories
- Set individual product bid budgets
- Monitor spending vs. budget
- Adjust budget allocation based on performance
- Plan seasonal budget changes

### Campaign Structure and Organization
- Organize campaigns by product type or category
- Group related products for management
- Plan campaign rotation by season
- Track promotion duration and timing
- Coordinate with inventory-management
- Monitor promotion effectiveness

### Performance Monitoring
- Track impressions, clicks, and CTR
- Monitor conversion rate and sales
- Calculate return on advertising spend (ROAS)
- Compare advertising ROI to organic sales
- Identify underperforming promotions
- Analyze traffic and sales patterns

### Optimization Strategy
- Pause promotions not meeting ROAS targets
- Increase bids on high-performing listings
- Test new products for promotion potential
- Rotate products based on seasonality
- Adjust budgets to high performers
- Implement continuous improvements

### Competitor and Market Analysis
- Monitor competitor advertising on Etsy
- Track competitive bidding levels
- Assess market visibility and positioning
- Identify market opportunities
- Evaluate seasonal competition changes
- Plan competitive response strategies

### Profitability Tracking
- Calculate true profitability of promoted listings
- Track advertising cost vs. incremental sales
- Monitor profit contribution by product
- Ensure ROI meets profitability targets
- Remove unprofitable promotions
- Report profitability to leadership

### Reporting and Analytics
- Generate weekly/monthly performance reports
- Track metrics by product and category
- Calculate advertising ROI and ROAS
- Identify trends and seasonal patterns
- Share insights and optimization recommendations
- Document decisions and results

## Success Metrics
- Return on ad spend (ROAS)
- Advertising cost of sale (ACoS)
- Total advertising revenue
- Profit per promoted product
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Conversion rate
