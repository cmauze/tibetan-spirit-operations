# Campaign Architecture Skill

## Overview
Establish overarching marketing strategy including channel selection, budget allocation, campaign planning, and cross-channel coordination.

## Key Sections

### Marketing Channel Strategy
- Evaluate all marketing channels (paid, organic, email, social)
- Assess customer journey and touchpoints
- Define role of each channel (awareness, consideration, conversion, retention)
- Prioritize channels by business objectives
- Plan channel mix and integration

### Budget Allocation Framework
- Establish total marketing budget
- Allocate budget across channels (paid vs. organic, by platform)
- Plan budget allocation by objective (awareness, conversion, retention)
- Balance testing/innovation vs. core performance
- Plan seasonal budget adjustments

### Channel-Specific Strategy
- Paid Search (Google Ads): High-intent capture, short-term ROI
- Paid Social (Meta, Pinterest, TikTok): Awareness, consideration, retargeting
- Amazon PPC: Product-focused conversion, competitive bidding
- Email/SMS: Retention, repeat purchase, high-value customer engagement
- Organic Search: Long-term visibility, content investment
- Organic Social: Community, engagement, brand affinity

### Campaign Planning Calendar
- Annual campaign calendar by channel and theme
- Seasonal campaign planning (aligned with tibetan-calendar)
- Product launch campaigns
- Promotional campaign planning
- Holiday and event campaigns

### Measurement and Analytics Framework
- Define KPIs by channel and campaign type
- Establish attribution and conversion tracking
- Plan A/B testing strategy across channels
- Set performance thresholds and benchmarks
- Plan regular reporting and review

### Audience Segmentation and Targeting
- Define core customer segments
- Develop segment-specific messaging strategies
- Plan channel allocation by segment
- Build lookalike and retargeting audiences
- Personalize messaging by segment

### Creative and Messaging Strategy
- Develop brand messaging guidelines
- Build core creative pillars and themes
- Plan messaging by campaign and audience
- Coordinate messaging across channels
- Establish approval process for creative

### Cross-Channel Coordination
- Plan customer journey across channels
- Coordinate timing of campaigns
- Manage frequency and exposure across channels
- Share audiences and data across channels
- Ensure consistent messaging

### Performance Management and Optimization
- Monitor performance against targets
- Implement optimization recommendations
- Conduct regular performance reviews
- Allocate budget to high-performing channels
- Plan scaling opportunities for success

### Forecasting and Planning
- Forecast revenue impact by channel
- Model ROI scenarios
- Plan quarterly adjustments
- Coordinate with product and promotion planning
- Build multi-year marketing strategy
