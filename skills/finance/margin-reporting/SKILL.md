# Margin Reporting Skill

## Overview
Systematically calculate and report on product margins and profitability by SKU, category, and channel to guide business decisions.

## Key Sections

### Margin Calculation Framework
- Define margin components and calculations
- Account for COGS (Cost of Goods Sold)
- Include fulfillment and shipping costs
- Add channel-specific fees (payment, platform, etc.)
- Calculate gross margin (revenue - COGS)
- Calculate net margin (after all costs)

### COGS Data Management
- Maintain accurate COGS by SKU
- Update COGS for supplier price changes
- Account for freight and duty costs
- Track landed cost including inbound freight
- Document COGS by sourcing location
- Monitor COGS trends and increases

### Channel Fee Analysis
- D2C Shopify: Payment processor fees only
- Amazon FBA: Referral fees, FBA fees, storage fees
- Etsy: Transaction, payment, shipping fees
- eBay: Insertion, final value, shipping fees
- Other channels: Calculate specific fee structures
- Update fee structures as they change

### Fulfillment Cost Allocation
- Assign fulfillment costs by SKU
- Account for fulfillment location and method
- Include inbound and outbound shipping
- Add handling and pick/pack costs
- Allocate storage costs
- Track fulfillment cost trends

### Weekly Margin Reports
- Calculate margins for all SKUs
- Report by category and subcategory
- Analyze by channel (D2C, Amazon, Etsy, etc.)
- Identify top performers and underperformers
- Highlight margin trends and changes
- Include week-over-week comparison

### SKU-Level Analysis
- Report margin by individual SKU
- Identify low-margin products
- Track margin changes over time
- Analyze margins by price point
- Compare similar products
- Recommend pricing or cost adjustments

### Category-Level Analysis
- Aggregate margins by product category
- Compare category profitability
- Identify category trends
- Analyze category mix and impact
- Report category contribution to profit
- Recommend category investments

### Channel Profitability
- Calculate profitability by channel
- Compare margin across channels
- Analyze channel-specific costs
- Identify most profitable channels
- Track channel profitability trends
- Recommend channel strategy adjustments

### Promotional Impact Analysis
- Calculate impact of promotions on margins
- Track margin during promotional periods
- Analyze promotional ROI
- Identify promotions that hurt margins
- Recommend promotional strategy adjustments
- Document promotion profitability

### Competitive Pricing Analysis
- Compare margins to competitor pricing
- Analyze pricing power by product
- Identify premium pricing opportunities
- Monitor margin compression from competition
- Plan pricing adjustments if needed
- Track pricing actions and results

### Reporting and Visualization
- Generate weekly margin reports
- Create dashboard for margin tracking
- Visualize margin trends
- Highlight key metrics and changes
- Provide easy-to-understand summaries
- Share with leadership and decision-makers

### Variance Analysis
- Compare actual to target margins
- Identify material variances
- Investigate root causes of changes
- Document variance explanations
- Recommend corrective actions
- Monitor implementation of actions

## Dependencies
- Integrates with: pricing-strategy, channel-profitability, promotion-strategy
- References: product-knowledge, inventory-management for cost data

## Success Metrics
- Average margin by SKU/category
- Margin trend (improving/declining)
- Variance from target margins
- Number of low-margin products
- Margin optimization recommendations implemented
- Profitability improvement from optimization
