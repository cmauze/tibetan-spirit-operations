# Channel Profitability Analysis Skill

## Overview
Analyze profitability across all sales channels accounting for all direct and indirect costs to determine channel value and guide strategy.

## Key Sections

### Channel Economics Framework
- Define channels: D2C, Amazon, Etsy, eBay, wholesale, other
- Identify all costs by channel type
- Calculate gross margin by channel
- Account for all channel-specific fees
- Calculate net profitability
- Compare economics across channels

### Channel-Specific Cost Structure
- D2C (Shopify): Platform fees, payment fees, customer acquisition, support
- Amazon: Referral fees, FBA fees, storage fees, packaging
- Etsy: Transaction fees, payment fees, advertising costs, shipping costs
- eBay: Insertion, final value fees, shipping reimbursement structures
- Wholesale: Sales commissions, bulk shipping, customer support
- Document fees and cost structures by channel

### Revenue Attribution by Channel
- Track sales by channel accurately
- Account for multi-channel customer journeys
- Assign revenue to primary purchase channel
- Track customer acquisition by channel
- Monitor channel-driven repeat purchases
- Analyze attribution models if applicable

### Direct Costs by Channel
- Calculate COGS for items sold by channel
- Add direct fulfillment costs
- Include payment processing fees
- Add platform/marketplace fees
- Include customer service costs by channel
- Track channel-specific packaging

### Indirect and Overhead Costs
- Allocate personnel time by channel
- Account for corporate overhead allocation
- Include technology and system costs
- Add marketing and advertising spend
- Include customer acquisition costs
- Allocate customer support costs

### Customer Lifetime Value by Channel
- Calculate LTV for customers acquired via each channel
- Track repeat purchase rates by channel
- Analyze customer retention by channel
- Calculate LTV:CAC ratio for each channel
- Monitor lifetime profitability by channel
- Use LTV for channel valuation

### Seasonal and Volume Analysis
- Analyze channel profitability by season
- Track impact of volume on unit economics
- Identify scale advantages by channel
- Monitor fixed cost absorption
- Plan capacity and inventory by channel
- Forecast volume impact on profitability

### Profitability Reporting
- Generate monthly channel profitability reports
- Calculate profit contribution by channel
- Report on profitability trends
- Compare channel performance
- Identify margin drivers and drains
- Present to leadership

### Channel Mix Optimization
- Analyze impact of channel mix on overall profitability
- Identify optimal channel allocation
- Evaluate expanding profitable channels
- Consider reducing low-margin channels
- Model impact of channel strategy changes
- Plan channel investment strategy

### Fee Impact Analysis
- Model impact of fee increases
- Analyze fee structure changes from platforms
- Plan price increases to offset fee hikes
- Negotiate fees when possible
- Plan channel tactics for fee mitigation
- Track cumulative fee impact on margins

### Competitive Positioning by Channel
- Monitor competitor channel strategy
- Analyze competitor profitability indicators
- Assess competitive advantages by channel
- Identify differentiation opportunities
- Plan competitive response strategies
- Track competitive pricing by channel

### Investment and Resource Allocation
- Allocate marketing budget by channel profitability
- Prioritize channel development investments
- Plan personnel allocation by channel
- Evaluate technology investments by channel
- Recommend resource reallocation
- Track ROI of channel investments

### Reporting and Decision Support
- Generate channel profitability comparison reports
- Present findings to leadership
- Provide recommendations for channel strategy
- Document assumptions and methodologies
- Maintain historical profitability data
- Support decision-making with data

## Dependencies
- Integrates with: margin-reporting, pricing-strategy, campaign-architecture
- References: inventory-management, fulfillment data for costs

## Success Metrics
- Profit by channel
- Profitability margin % by channel
- Customer lifetime value by channel
- Return on channel investment
- Channel contribution to total profit
- Profitability trend by channel
