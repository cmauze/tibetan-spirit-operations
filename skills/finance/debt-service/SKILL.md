# Debt Service and Loan Repayment Skill

## Overview
Manage investor loan repayment schedule, ensure timely payments, and track debt obligations to maintain investor relationships and financial health.

## Key Sections

### Loan Terms and Documentation
- Document loan principal amount and terms
- Maintain loan agreement and documentation
- Track interest rate and calculation method
- Document repayment schedule and timeline
- Maintain loan covenant requirements
- Record disbursement history

### Repayment Schedule Management
- Maintain detailed repayment schedule
- Identify payment due dates
- Calculate payment amounts by period
- Track principal and interest portions
- Plan payment timing and amounts
- Coordinate with cash flow planning

### Payment Tracking
- Record each loan payment made
- Document payment date and amount
- Track principal reduction over time
- Monitor interest paid
- Reconcile payments to schedule
- Document payment confirmations

### Cash Flow Planning
- Forecast loan payment requirements
- Plan cash availability for payments
- Monitor cash flow impact
- Coordinate payments with business cash generation
- Ensure adequate reserves for payments
- Plan seasonal adjustments to cash flow

### Interest Calculation and Documentation
- Verify interest calculations
- Document interest accrual method
- Track cumulative interest paid
- Calculate remaining interest obligation
- Monitor interest vs. principal paydown
- Ensure accurate accounting

### Covenant Compliance
- Monitor loan covenants and requirements
- Track financial ratios and metrics
- Ensure compliance with restrictions
- Document covenant compliance status
- Alert on potential covenant violations
- Plan for covenant test timing

### Investor Communication
- Keep investor informed of repayment status
- Provide periodic payment statements
- Report on financial performance
- Address investor questions and concerns
- Maintain positive investor relationships
- Document all communications

### Accounting and Financial Reporting
- Record loan payments in accounting system
- Allocate payments between principal and interest
- Track debt on balance sheet
- Report on debt levels and ratios
- Calculate debt service coverage ratio
- Ensure compliance with accounting standards

### Payment Coordination
- Coordinate payment timing with cash flow
- Execute payments on schedule
- Confirm payment receipts with lender
- Address any payment issues promptly
- Maintain payment documentation
- Track payment method and processing fees

### Forecasting and Prepayment Analysis
- Forecast remaining debt balance
- Calculate total remaining interest
- Analyze prepayment options if available
- Model impact of early repayment
- Plan optimal repayment strategy
- Consider refinancing opportunities

## Dependencies
- Integrates with: margin-reporting, channel-profitability
- References: financial records and agreements

## Success Metrics
- Payment timeliness (% on-time)
- Covenant compliance status
- Debt reduction progress
- Interest costs
- Debt service coverage ratio
