# Amazon Fee Analysis Skill

## Overview
Systematically calculate and track all Amazon fees to understand true profitability and identify optimization opportunities.

## Key Sections

### Amazon Fee Types
- Referral Fee: % of selling price (category-dependent, typically 8-15%)
- FBA Fee: Per-unit fee based on weight and size tier
- Storage Fee: Monthly per-unit fee for inventory in FC
- High-Volume Listing Fee: Applied to low-volume listings
- Return Processing Fee: Charged for customer returns
- Other fees: Removal, disposal, oversize handling, etc.

### Referral Fee Calculation
- Identify applicable referral fee rate by category
- Calculate fee on final selling price including tax
- Monitor referral fee changes by Amazon
- Account for reduced referral fees in some categories
- Track referral fees by product
- Sum total referral fees by period

### FBA Fee Analysis
- Identify product weight and size dimensions
- Classify product into size tier (standard, oversize, large oversize)
- Look up applicable FBA fee per unit
- Calculate total FBA fees for unit quantity
- Track FBA fee changes
- Monitor impact of size on profitability

### Storage Fee Calculation
- Track inventory levels in Amazon FCs
- Calculate storage fee per unit per month
- Monitor peak season storage fee impact
- Identify products with high holding costs
- Plan inventory reduction for high storage cost items
- Analyze storage fee impact on profitability

### Cost Per Unit Analysis
- Calculate total Amazon fees per unit sold
- Combine referral, FBA, and other applicable fees
- Compare to product price and gross margin
- Identify products with high fee burden
- Track fee per unit trends
- Compare fee cost across product categories

### Profitability Impact
- Calculate product margin after Amazon fees
- Identify products with low profit after fees
- Analyze profitability by price point
- Calculate minimum selling price for profitability
- Track profitability impact of fee changes
- Identify optimization opportunities

### Fee Optimization Strategies
- Identify underperforming listings for potential delisting
- Analyze benefit of reducing product size/weight
- Evaluate alternative packaging for lower weight
- Consider removing ASIN or consolidating SKUs
- Plan pricing adjustments for fee recovery
- Test price increases impact on volume

### Inventory Management for Fees
- Monitor inventory levels and storage fees
- Plan fulfillment quantities to minimize storage
- Coordinate with inventory-management for optimization
- Plan inventory reduction for high-fee seasons
- Monitor stranded inventory and removal fees
- Calculate inventory carrying cost

### Seasonal Fee Impact
- Analyze storage fee impact during peak season
- Monitor fluctuations in inventory levels
- Plan inventory builds for peak season
- Monitor fee impact on cash flow
- Coordinate with assortment-planning for timing
- Forecast fee impact by season

### Competitive Pricing Accounting
- Analyze competitor pricing relative to fees
- Account for fee burden in pricing decisions
- Coordinate with pricing-strategy
- Monitor price erosion due to fees
- Plan promotional pricing around fee impact
- Calculate sustainable pricing levels

### Reporting and Analysis
- Generate Amazon fee impact reports
- Calculate fees as % of revenue
- Track fee trends over time
- Compare fee impact by category
- Report on fee optimization results
- Present findings to leadership

### Fee Forecasting and Planning
- Forecast expected Amazon fees by product
- Plan budget allocation for fees
- Monitor actual vs. forecast fees
- Analyze variance from expectations
- Plan for fee increases announced by Amazon
- Build contingency for unexpected fee changes
