# Nepal Payments and Exchange Tracking Skill

## Overview
Manage supplier payments in Nepali Rupees, track exchange rates for USD/NPR conversions, and reconcile international transactions accurately.

## Key Sections

### Supplier Payment Coordination
- Coordinate with supplier-communication for PO amounts
- Track payment obligations by supplier
- Schedule payments according to payment terms
- Maintain supplier payment history
- Document payment amounts and dates
- Track outstanding invoices and aging

### NPR Exchange Rate Monitoring
- Monitor USD to NPR exchange rates
- Track historical rate trends
- Identify optimal payment timing for rates
- Document rates used for transactions
- Alert on significant rate movements
- Plan forward contracts if applicable

### Payment Method and Processing
- Determine payment method (wire, bank transfer, other)
- Calculate payment amounts in NPR from USD costs
- Arrange bank transfers to suppliers
- Track payment confirmations
- Maintain payment documentation
- Document payment processing fees

### Invoice Reconciliation
- Match supplier invoices to POs
- Verify invoice amounts and details
- Check for discrepancies or issues
- Reconcile exchange rates used
- Approve invoices for payment
- Track invoice approval workflow

### Currency Conversion
- Calculate USD to NPR conversions at payment time
- Document exchange rate used
- Calculate landed costs including FX impact
- Track FX gains/losses
- Update cost tracking with actual rates
- Analyze FX impact on margins

### Payment Documentation
- Maintain records of all payments
- Document supporting invoices
- Keep payment confirmations
- Track wire transfer confirmations
- Maintain payment reports
- Organize by supplier and date

### Accounting Integration
- Record payments in accounting system
- Track accounts payable by supplier
- Reconcile with general ledger
- Document currency conversion adjustments
- Track FX gains or losses
- Ensure proper coding for expenses

### Supplier Relationship Management
- Communicate payment status to suppliers
- Confirm payment receipt with suppliers
- Address any payment discrepancies
- Track payment performance history
- Plan future payment arrangements
- Document any payment adjustments

### Reporting and Analytics
- Generate supplier payment reports
- Track payment terms compliance
- Monitor FX impact on costs
- Calculate landed costs including FX
- Report on cash flow timing
- Analyze payment patterns

### Forecasting and Planning
- Forecast upcoming supplier payments
- Plan cash requirements for payments
- Monitor cash flow impact
- Coordinate with inventory planning
- Plan seasonal payment fluctuations
- Build payment reserves if needed
