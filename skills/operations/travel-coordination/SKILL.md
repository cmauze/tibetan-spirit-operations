# Travel Coordination Skill

## Overview
Coordinate pilgrimage trips through TS Travels, managing scheduling, itineraries, participant logistics, and trip documentation.

## Key Sections

### Trip Planning and Scheduling
- Identify pilgrimage destinations and timing
- Align with tibetan-calendar (Losar, Vesak, seasonal events)
- Determine trip duration and dates
- Plan seasonal trips aligned with spiritual significance

### Itinerary Development
- Research pilgrimage sites and attractions
- Build detailed day-by-day itineraries
- Include meditation sessions, ceremonies, or spiritual practices
- Plan logistics between sites

### Participant Management
- Track registrations and enrollments
- Gather participant information and preferences
- Manage dietary restrictions and accessibility needs
- Confirm participant commitments

### Logistics Coordination
- Arrange flights and ground transportation
- Book accommodations aligned with pilgrimage nature
- Coordinate meal arrangements
- Arrange travel insurance

### Documentation and Communication
- Prepare travel guides and pre-trip materials
- Communicate itinerary and logistics to participants
- Provide payment information and deadlines
- Send pre-departure reminders and guidance

### On-Trip Support
- Coordinate with on-site logistics
- Manage emergency contacts and communications
- Handle participant issues during travel
- Ensure spiritual practices are honored

### Post-Trip Follow-up
- Gather feedback from participants
- Document lessons learned
- Plan improvements for future trips
- Thank participants and maintain relationships

## Dependencies
- References: tibetan-calendar for spiritual timing
- External: TS Travels partnership

## Success Metrics
- Trips completed on schedule
- Participant satisfaction
- Spiritual objectives achieved
- Repeat participant engagement
