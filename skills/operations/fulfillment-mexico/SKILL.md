# Fulfillment - Mexico Operations Skill

## Overview
Manage Latin American sales fulfillment through partnership with Espíritu Tibetano and Omar, including order routing, handoff, and delivery tracking.

## Key Sections

### Sales Channel Monitoring
- Monitor orders flagged for Mexico/Latin America shipping
- Identify customers in Mexico, Central America, South America
- Determine if order should route through Omar

### Order Handoff to Omar
- Prepare order details for Omar's fulfillment
- Communicate order info and delivery expectations
- Track handoff completion

### Inventory Coordination
- Confirm inventory availability for Latin America shipments
- Arrange pre-positioning of products with Omar if needed
- Track stock levels in Omar's location

### Shipping and Delivery
- Obtain tracking information from Omar's fulfillment
- Monitor delivery progress
- Share tracking with customer

### Customer Communication
- Provide accurate delivery timelines to customer
- Manage expectations around transit times
- Facilitate any customer inquiries with Omar

### Performance Tracking
- Monitor fulfillment speed and success rates
- Track delivery accuracy and customer satisfaction
- Document any issues for improvement

## Dependencies
- Integrates with: order-inquiry, inventory-management
- External partner: Espíritu Tibetano / Omar

## Success Metrics
- Orders successfully routed to Omar
- Delivery tracking provided to customers
- On-time delivery rate
- Customer satisfaction
