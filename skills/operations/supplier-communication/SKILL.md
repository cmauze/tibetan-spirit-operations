# Supplier Communication Skill

## Overview
Serve as primary communication hub with Nepal suppliers, managing purchase orders, inquiries, and correspondence in appropriate languages.

## Key Sections

### Supplier Relationship Management
- Maintain relationships with Nepal-based suppliers
- Document supplier contact information and terms
- Track supplier performance and reliability

### Purchase Order Management
- Draft POs with clear specifications and quantities
- Include delivery dates, pricing, and payment terms
- Track PO status and supplier responses
- Manage PO changes and amendments

### Correspondence in English
- Communicate with suppliers in English
- Handle inquiries, clarifications, and issues
- Maintain professional communication records

### Correspondence in Bahasa Indonesia
- Manage communications with Jhoti in Bahasa Indonesia
- Facilitate any special requests or bulk orders for Jhoti
- Clarify specifications and delivery requirements

### Order Tracking
- Monitor shipment status from order to delivery
- Track expected arrival dates
- Identify delays and coordinate solutions
- Manage any customs or logistics issues

### Pricing and Payment Coordination
- Negotiate pricing for new orders
- Manage payment terms and arrangements
- Track supplier invoices and payment records
- Monitor currency exchange impacts

### Issue Resolution
- Address supplier issues (late shipments, quality, etc.)
- Coordinate returns or replacements
- Escalate persistent problems

## Dependencies
- Integrates with: fulfillment-nepal, inventory-management
- External: Nepal suppliers, Jhoti

## Success Metrics
- Timely PO placement and confirmation
- On-time supplier delivery
- Accurate order fulfillment
- Positive supplier relationships
