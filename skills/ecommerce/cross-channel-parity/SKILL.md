# Cross-Channel Parity Skill

## Overview
Systematically audit and maintain consistency of product information, pricing, and descriptions across all sales channels to ensure customer trust and data integrity.

## Key Sections

### Channel Listing Audit
- Compare product listings across D2C (Shopify), Etsy, and Amazon
- Verify product titles match or are appropriately optimized per channel
- Check descriptions are consistent or channel-adapted
- Confirm images are current across all channels

### Pricing Consistency Check
- Compare prices across channels
- Account for channel-specific fees and margins
- Verify promotional pricing is synchronized
- Check for pricing conflicts or arbitrage opportunities

### Product Information Accuracy
- Verify SKU/ASIN mappings are correct
- Check product categories are appropriate per channel
- Confirm attributes and variants are listed correctly
- Validate product tags and keywords

### Stock Level Synchronization
- Compare inventory levels across channels
- Identify inventory sync issues
- Verify no overselling across channels
- Monitor for stock imbalances

### Change Management
- Track when updates were made to each channel
- Verify updates propagated correctly
- Identify any failed syncs or delays
- Document manual updates

### Issue Detection and Resolution
- Flag listing inconsistencies for review
- Identify stale or outdated information
- Resolve pricing conflicts
- Correct data entry errors

### Reporting and Analytics
- Generate parity audit reports
- Track consistency scores by channel and category
- Identify recurring issues
- Recommend process improvements

## Dependencies
- Integrates with: inventory-management, content-performance, etsy-content-optimization, amazon-listing-optimization
- Monitors: Shopify, Etsy, Amazon platforms

## Success Metrics
- Listing parity score by channel
- Price consistency percentage
- Time to detect discrepancies
- Sync failure rate
