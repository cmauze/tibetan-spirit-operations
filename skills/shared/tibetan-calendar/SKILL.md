# Tibetan Calendar and Seasonal Events Skill

## Overview
Maintain and reference the Tibetan lunar calendar and significant spiritual events to align business activities, marketing, and product planning with seasonal significance.

## Key Sections

### Calendar Events and Dates
- Losar (Tibetan New Year): Typically January/February - major celebration period
- Vesak (Buddha's Birthday): Typically April/May - important Buddhist holy day
- Saga Dawa (Buddha's Enlightenment Month): Typically May/June
- Yogurt Festival: Typically August in Tibet
- Additional regional and school-specific events
- Important fast days and practice periods

### 2026 Key Dates Reference
- Document exact dates for all major events for the current year
- Note regional variations in celebration dates
- Track lunar calendar conversion to Gregorian calendar
- Maintain rolling calendar for upcoming years
- Flag critical dates for planning
- Share with team for alignment
