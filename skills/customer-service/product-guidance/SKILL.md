# Product Guidance Skill

## Overview
Assist customers in selecting appropriate products from the Tibetan Spirit catalog based on their practice level, spiritual goals, preferences, and budget.

## Key Sections

### Customer Assessment
- Identify practice level (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
- Understand specific spiritual intentions
- Note any preferences or restrictions (dietary, scent sensitivities, etc.)

### Product Recommendation Engine
- Load product-knowledge database
- Match customer needs to product attributes
- Consider practice type, quality tier, and value

### Guidance Conversation
- Recommend 2-3 products with clear reasoning
- Explain differences and use cases
- Provide context on how each product supports practice

### Purchase Facilitation
- Address questions about benefits and authenticity
- Share pricing and availability
- Guide to checkout or suggest related items

## Dependencies
- Loads: product-knowledge skill for full catalog and background

## Success Metrics
- Customer selects a product
- Customer expresses confidence in choice
- Positive feedback on recommendation quality
